# MAIA - Web3 Real-World Asset Investment Platform

MAIA transforms high-potential real-world projects into transparent, tokenized investment opportunities within a gamified territorial expansion ecosystem. We bridge physical assets with blockchain technology, creating structured access to emerging market opportunities for global investors.

## Core Features

- **Tokenized RWA**: Convert real-world assets (solar energy, agriculture, infrastructure) into tradeable NFTs
- **Territorial Gamification**: Strategic expansion mechanics where investors become architects of economic growth
- **AI Analytics Layer**: Predictive modeling, risk scoring, and scenario simulation for informed decision-making
- **DAO Governance**: Community-driven governance affecting project selection, territorial expansion, and strategic direction
- **Transparent Trust Infrastructure**: Multi-layer verification, third-party audits, and on-chain transparency

## Tech Stack

- Smart Contracts (ERC-721 NFTs, Governance)
- AI/ML Engine for analytics and forecasting
- Hexagonal territory visualization system
- IPFS for decentralized data storage
- Multi-signature custody mechanisms

## Philosophy

*"Where capital touches the land again"* - MAIA creates meaningful connections between digital finance and tangible asset value through sophisticated gamification and transparent governance structures.
